Project Overview

When the world of commerce started, people would barter ( trading objects for other objects ) but there was no probability in trade. To
appear the first form of portable currency was in Moneta seashells. MonetaPRO was founded with the vision of principle portability in
trade.

MonetaPro is a B2B marketplace for an existing $77T market (Corporate Barter) • Utilize an Asset Earned Stable Coin called a USD-G, which acts as an internal store credit for noncash trading • The MON Token is a qualified and legally certified VFA Token (Malta), and trades outside of the marketplace • For the investor- there is a
dual liquidity option: A- MON swap to a exchange to a $7 USD-G, or B- Trade MON on exchanges
